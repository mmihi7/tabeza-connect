📋 How it works

The template generator needs 3 sample receipts from your POS to learn the format:

  1. Print 3 different receipts from your POS (different items, prices)
  2. Tabeza Connect will capture each receipt automatically
  3. AI will analyze the receipts and create a parsing template
  4. Future receipts will be parsed automatically using this template
